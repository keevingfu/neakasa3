TikTok Shop Drives Growth
TikTok Shop, launched this year, already generates 86.7% of total revenue with impressive MoM growth of 89%. The channel shows strong momentum with AOV increasing 30% MoM to $187.02.
Strategic Recommendation
• Accelerate TikTok Shop expansion given the exceptional growth trajectory
• Invest in TikTok-native content creators to maintain momentum
• Develop exclusive product bundles to further increase AOV
• Establish backup accounts to mitigate suspension risks
Impressive YoY Revenue Growth
Overall revenue grew 287% YoY, significantly outpacing order growth (215%), indicating successful AOV optimization. The growth is primarily driven by new channel addition (TikTok Shop) and improved channel mix.
Strategic Recommendation
• Continue AOV optimization strategies across all channels
• Replicate TikTok Shop's premium positioning to other channels
• Focus on quality over quantity to maintain growth trajectory
• Set aggressive but achievable targets for next year (+150% YoY)
Channel Performance Divergence
While DTC shows strong YoY growth (167% revenue, 189% orders), Amazon struggles with declining orders (-20% YoY) despite traffic growth. The conversion rate decline (-74% MoM) requires immediate attention.
Strategic Recommendation
• Conduct urgent Amazon listing optimization and competitive analysis
• Implement Amazon PPC campaigns to improve visibility
• Review pricing strategy to ensure competitiveness
• Consider Amazon-exclusive promotions to boost conversion
MoM Volatility Concerns
Despite strong YoY growth, MoM metrics show volatility: orders declined 12% and conversion rate dropped 29%. This suggests potential seasonality or campaign effectiveness issues requiring investigation.
Strategic Recommendation
• Analyze seasonal patterns to better predict performance fluctuations
• Implement consistent month-over-month tracking and early warning systems
• Diversify traffic sources to reduce volatility
• Create buffer inventory for high-demand periods
